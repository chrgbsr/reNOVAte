The Problem

Nova Launcher, once the most popular Android launcher, has gone silent. No updates, no communication, no roadmap. The app that defined Android customization is slowly becoming incompatible.

02

The Idea

What if the community took over? An open-source fork, maintained by developers who actually use it, built in the open with contributions from anyone who cares.

03

The Movement

reNOVAtion isn't just an app — it's a statement. That great software shouldn't die because a company moves on. That open source is the answer.
